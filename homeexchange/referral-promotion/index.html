effect on churn

Query
BigQuery SQL · referral_promotion_churn.sql
SELECT
  ever_referred,
  total_promotions > 0                        AS had_promotion,
  COUNT(*)                                   AS total_customers,
  ROUND(COUNTIF(is_churned = 1) * 100.0
    / COUNT(*), 1)                          AS churn_rate_pct
FROM `lewagon-457508`.home_exchange_reporting.v_customer_activity
GROUP BY 1, 2
ORDER BY 1, 2
Key findings
Promotion is the strongest lever
A promotional discount reduces churn by nearly 40 percentage points — the single biggest retention signal in the dataset
Referral builds loyalty
Referred customers churn at 34.2% vs 46.8% baseline — word-of-mouth brings more committed members
Combined effect is near-zero churn
Referred customers who also received a promotion churn at just 4.9% — but this group is small (349 customers)
Scale the referral programme
Only 6,388 referred customers out of 70,726 total (9%). Growing this channel while adding promotional incentives could dramatically improve retention
